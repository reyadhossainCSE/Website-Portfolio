## ğŸ“ How to Customize

### 1. Personal Information
Edit `index.html` and replace the following placeholders:
- **"Your Name"** - Replace with your actual name
- **Email addresses** - Update with your contact email
- **Social media links** - Add your LinkedIn, GitHub, ResearchGate, Google Scholar links
- **Location** - Update your university and location details
- **Office details** - Add your office building and room number

### 2. About Me Section
Update the paragraphs in the "About Me" section with your personal story and research interests.

### 3. Research Projects
Replace the three project cards with your actual research projects:
- Update project titles
- Add project descriptions
- Change project images (use URLs from Unsplash, Pexels, or your own hosted images)
- Update tags/keywords
- Add links to project pages or papers

### 4. Skills
Adjust the skill percentages and names to match your actual expertise:
- Research Skills section
- Technical Skills section
- Additional skills icons

### 5. Experience
Update the timeline with your actual work experience:
- Position titles
- Institution names
- Dates
- Descriptions
- Skills/tags

### 6. Education
Replace with your actual education details:
- Degree names
- University names
- Graduation/expected completion dates
- Research focus areas
- Achievements and honors

### 7. Profile Image
Replace the placeholder image in the About section:
```html
<img src="YOUR_IMAGE_URL_HERE" alt="Profile">
```

### 8. CV Upload
To enable actual CV download:
1. Upload your CV PDF to the website folder
2. Update the "Download CV" button link to point to your CV file
